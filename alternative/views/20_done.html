Your claim for loss of notice has been submitted

What you can do next

  • If you were self employed please send a copy of your Trading, profit and loss account to us, labelling it carefully with your name, address and National Insurance number
  • Please contact us if you have changed your bank account since you were paid your redundancy or insolvency payments
  • Send email or post copies of your evidence to the Redundancy Payments service. We accept clear scans, digital images and photocopies. Include your name, address and National Insurance number
  • If you have any questions about your claim, contact the Redundancy Payments Service quoting your National Insurance Number
