Debt help

Being in debt can be stressful and overwhelming but there are steps you can take to help get yourself out of debt.

The information below is an impartial overview of 4 common debt solutions that could help you. The purpose is to help you learn about your options so you can choose the right solution for you.

Debt Relief Order

Clear up to £15,000 of debt you can’t repay. It’s meant for those with few assets and little extra income.

Government service Protection from creditors Write off debt 1 year duration A £90 one off charge Affects credit history for 6 years
Check eligibility and find out more  

Bankruptcy

An Official Receiver manages payment of your debts using your assets and extra income. If you have no assets and no surplus income you will not be asked to repay.

Government service Possible Monthly repayment Assets at risk Protection from creditors Write off debt 1-3 years duration A £705 one off charge Affects credit history for 6 years
Check eligibility and find out more  

Individual Voluntary Arrangements

Work with an Insolvency Practitioner to pay your debts using your assets and extra income.

Commercial service Monthly repayment Assets at risk Protection from creditors Write off debt 5 years duration Up front fee and additional fees Affects credit history for 10 years
Find out more  

Debt management plans

Work with a debt management company to arrange lower monthly payments.

Commercial service Monthly repayment Assets at risk Variable duration Up front fee and additional fees Affects credit history during plan
