Do you have any cash or assets?

Answer Yes to this question if you have any of the following and they add up to £300 or more :

  • Surplus cash in your bank account after your living expenses?
  • Any savings
  • If you own items other than those satisfying basic needs such as your washing machine, or tools of the trade such as machinery and equipment
