Before you start

The bankruptcy application has eight sections.

Having information about each to hand before you begin should make it easier to complete your application.

We will ask you about:

1. Personal details

  • your personal details, including your address, contact information and details about who lives with you

2. Employment

  • your employment history over the last 12 months, including the names and addresses of all your employers
  • any self employed businesses you have had in the last 3 years, including your trading details, trading addresses, business partners, employees and accountants

3. Banks and savings

  • all bank and savings accounts in your name, including account numbers, sort codes and account balances

4. Assets

  • properties you have owned in the last 3 years, including property addresses, mortgage details and any secured loans
  • vehicles you have owned or used regularly in the last 12 months, including the make, the model and any outstanding financing
  • possessions worth over £500 each, including electronics, jewellery, bicycles, etc
  • any other assets you have, including trusts, stocks, shares, legal claims and compensations
  • any pensions, endowments or life insurance policies in your name

5. Money you owe

  • all of your creditors, including how much they say you owe and whether they have taken any action against you

6. Income and expenses

  • your income from all sources, including wages, pensions and benefits
  • your entire household’s income, including wages or benefits anyone you live with receives
  • your contribution to your household’s expenses, including groceries, childcare, travel, etc

7. Legal proceedings

  • any solicitors you have used in the last 5 years, including the date you used them and their address
  • any divorces, legal separations or dissolutions you have been involved in over the last 5 years, including the court where you sent the forms, the court reference number and the date the processed finished
  • any ongoing claims any have made to a court, tribunal, ombudsman or adjudicator, including the reference number
  • any ongoing claims made against you to a court, tribunal, ombudsman or adjudicator, including the reference number

8. Debt history

  • how and when your debts started
  • debt management options you have tried in the last 2 years, including debt management plans (DMPs), individual voluntary arrangements (IVAs), debt relief orders (DROs), etc
  • Extra payments you have made to some creditors but not others (called preferential payments) in last 2 years, including the creditors’ names, the payment amount and the date it was made

 

This isn’t an all-inclusive list, and we may ask you for other information as well.

You can find out more about the bankruptcy process on GOV.UK.
