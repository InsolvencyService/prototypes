Applying for bankruptcy:

What you need to complete the application

  1. 1. Personal information
  2. 2. Trading information
  3. 3. Bank details
  4. 4. Assets
  1. 5. Money you owe
  2. 6. Income and outgoings
  3. 7. Legal action
  4. 8. Your debt history
1

Personal information

  • your full name
  • date of birth
  • your current address (if renting, your landlords’ name and address)
  • contact details
  • relationship status
  • details of previous addresses
  • names you're known by
  • your national insurance number
  • your current employment details
  • who lives with you
  • details of any dependents
2

Trading information

If you have been self-employed you will need to provide:

  • information about your business name
  • trading address
  • trading start and end dates
  • VAT and tax references
  • details of any employees working for you and previous employees who are still owed monies by you
  • accountants details
  • trading partners

If you have been a company director you will need to provide information about these companies.

3

Trading information

If you have been self-employed you will need to provide:

  • details of bank account numbers and sort codes in your name, including joint accounts
  • information about the money held in each account
  • when each account was opened
4

Assets

You will need to provide detailed information about any assets you own, such as:

  • a property, including details of mortgagees and charge holders and you are a landlord details about the tenant and letting agent
  • a vehicle, including make, model, registration number, current value and details about any finance agreement in place
  • a pension or life policy – we will need the provider name and address, policy numbers and details of beneficiaries under the policy
  • stocks and shares – name of investment and approximate value
  • details of anyone who owes you money
  • details or any material assets sold, gifted or transferred in the last 5 years

You will need to provide account numbers and information about the value of the asset.

5

Money you owe

You will need to provide information about all the people you owe money to, including:

  • full names and addresses
  • account numbers
  • the amount you owe
  • The date the debt started
6

Information about your income and outgoings

  • our household monthly income and expenses
7

Legal action

  • any legal proceedings you are involved in, including the contact details for your solicitor, court details and reference numbers
8

Your debt history

  • you will need to provide information about how and when your debt problems started and anything you have done to try to manage your debt, such as a debt management plan or IVA
