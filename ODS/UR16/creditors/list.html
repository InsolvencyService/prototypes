Money you owe

Organisations and people you owe money to are known as creditors.


What we need to know

We need to know about all of your creditors, including family members or friends.


If a debt collection agency is involved:

  • check letters from your creditor to see if your debt has been sold to the agency
  • for sold debts, we only need to know about the debt collection agency
  • for all other debts, we only need to know about your original creditor
Don't include any debts you have already told us about (eg mortgages, car financing or overdrafts).
