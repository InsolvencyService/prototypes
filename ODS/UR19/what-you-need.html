Applying for bankruptcy:

What the application covers

  1. 1. Personal details
  2. 2. Employment
  3. 3. Bank accounts and savings
  4. 4. Assets
  1. 5. Money you owe
  2. 6. Income and expenses
  3. 7. Legal proceedings
  4. 8. Debt history
1

Personal details

  • your full name
  • date of birth
  • contact details
  • relationship status
  • National Insurance number
  • other names you're known by
  • your current address and any previous addresses from the last 3 years
  • if renting, your landlord's name and address
  • who lives with you
  • details of any dependants
2

Employment

If you have been employed you will need to provide:

  • details of all your employers over the last 12 months

If you have been self-employed you will need to provide:

  • details of self-employed businesses you have had in the last 3 years (eg sole trader)
  • details of non-limited partnerships you have had in the last 3 years
  • trading addresses
  • trading start and end dates
  • VAT and tax references
  • details of any employees working for you and previous employees who claim you owe them money
  • details of any accountants, financial advisers or bookkeepers you have used in the last 5 years
  • names and addresses of any trading/business partners

If you have been a director of a limited company you will need to provide:

  • details of any companies were you have held this post over the last 12 months
3

Bank accounts and savings

  • all bank and building society accounts in your name
  • bank/building society addresses
  • account numbers and sort codes
  • account balances
  • joint account holders
4

Assets

If you own any properties you'll need to provide:

  • property address
  • description of the property (number of bedrooms, ownership type, etc)
  • who lives there and if they pay rent
  • current property value
  • joint property owners
  • details of any mortgages and secured loans

If you own, have recently sold or regularly use any vehicles you'll need to provide:

  • vehicle make and model
  • registration number
  • current vehicle value
  • address where the vehicle is kept overnight
  • details of any financing (hire purchase, etc)

If there are any you pension or insurance policies in your name you'll need to provide:

  • policy provider details
  • policy type and number
  • date you took out the policy
  • current policy value
  • details of any payments you're receiving

For your other assets and possessions you'll need to provide a description and a current value for:

  • any cash you have
  • items or possession worth more than £500 each (TV, computer, furniture, bicycles, etc)
  • compensations or legal claims in your name
  • trusts or wills you receive/will receive money from
  • stocks, shares and investments in your name
  • self-employed business assets
  • any money you're owed
5

Money you owe

You will need to provide information about all the people you owe money to (your creditors), including:

  • full names and addresses
  • account numbers/debt reference numbers
  • amounts your creditors say you owe them
  • details of any debt collection agencies who have bought your debts
  • the date each debt started
  • details of any assets your creditors have taken control of
  • details of any attachment of earnings against you
6

Income and expenses

  • breakdown of your monthly income (salary/wage details, benefits, etc)
  • the amount you pay in income tax (PAYE or self assessment)
  • the rest of your household's income from their jobs, benefits and pensions
  • breakdown of how much you contribute to your household's living costs, including:
    • home and utilities (rent/mortgage, council tax, electric, water, etc)
    • pension, insurance and healthcare
    • food and housekeeping
    • transport and travel
    • childcare and maintenance
    • personal and leisure
    • regular payments into savings
    • fines and other expenses
7

Legal proceedings

  • legal separations, divorces or dissolutions you have been involved in over the last 5 years, including court details and reference numbers
  • ongoing legal claims you're currently involved in, including court details and reference numbers
  • details of any solicitors you have used in the last 5 years
8

Debt history

  • when and how your debts started
  • debt management options you have tried in the last 2 years (debt management plans, debt relief orders, etc)
  • details of any extra payments you have made to some creditors but not others (preferential payments) in the last 2 years
