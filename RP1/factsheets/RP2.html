Completing your loss of notice claim online

When completing your claim for a redundancy or insolvency payment you told us you also want to claim compensation for loss of notice. 

Your statutory notice period has now come to an end and we need to know about any income during your notice period in order to process your claim.

You can now complete your claim online. The online claim is an updated version of the enclosed paper form. Online claims are paid on average 5 days quicker than paper claims.

What you need to make a claim online

To complete your claim online you need:

  • your email address
  • details of any money you earned during the notice period
  • details of any benefit payments you received, or were entitled to, during the notice period

To start a claim

The claim reference is at the top right of the paper form
  1. Visit http://rp1-pre.redundancy-payments.org.uk/claims/start
  2. Click Start now
  3. Enter your National Insurance number
  4. Enter the claim reference beginning with L (this can be found on the paper form)

Completing your claim

Completing a claim takes between 10 and 20 minutes.

If you don’t enter any information for more than 30 minutes, your claim will time out and you’ll need to start again.

Receiving your payment

Any earnings, benefits or increases in benefits during your notice period will be deducted. The maximum statutory notice entitlement, after these deductions, is £464 per week.

We aim to pay 80% of claims within 21 days.

If you’re unable to get online

If you’re having difficulty getting online you can:

  • ask someone you know to help
  • check if your local library has internet access or call 0800 771 234 to find your nearest internet location
  • call us on 0330 331 0020 (select option 2)

If you don’t wish to complete your claim online you can still use the enclosed paper form. Send it to the address on the back of the form.

Contact us

Please quote your case reference (eg CN12345678) or National Insurance number when contacting us.

  • Email:  redundancypaymentsonline@insolvency.gsi.gov.uk
  • Telephone: 0330 331 0020 (select option 2). Opening hours are 9am to 5pm, Monday to Friday.
