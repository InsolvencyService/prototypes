Claim for redundancy and monies owed

Your redundancy claim has been received and is being processed

You will be contacted when your registration is confirmed. Until then your name will not appear on the electoral register.

We have sent you a confirmation email.

What happens next

Your Insolvency Practitioner will give us more details about your claim to help us calculate the amount you’re owed. We aim to pay you within 3 to 6 weeks.

Making a claim for loss of notice

Based on the dates you’ve given, your statutory notice period is from 01 March 2015 to 30 April 2015.

You’ll receive a letter or email asking you to complete your claim after 30 April 2015. The letter or email may take up to 6 weeks to arrive.

If you don’t claim for Jobseeker's Allowance your payment for loss of notice will be affected.

What we need from you

You need to send the item(s) below. Please include your name and claim reference number.

  • You need to send us a copy of your employment tribunal judgement.
  • You need to send either a copy of your letter to your employer or the employment tribunal.
  • As you are a sub contractor, you need to complete an RP4 form to enable us to check whether you qualify as an employed as defined by legislation. We will include an RP4 form with the email we send you.
  • Are you are a director of a company, you need to complete an RP3 form to enable us to check whether you qualify as an employed as defined by legislation. We will include an RP3 form with the email we send you.

You can email photos or scans of your documents to redundancypaymentsonline@insolvency.gsi.gov.uk

What you can do next

  • Get advice on finding a new job
  • Apply for Jobseeker's Allowance
  • Ask for your Rapid Response Service at your local Jobcentre Plus. They should tell you more about training opportunities.
