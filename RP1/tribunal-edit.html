Other questions about your claim

Have you been awarded any payments in relation to your job ending by an Employment tribunal?

If you answered yes, you need to send us a copy of your employment tribunal judgement. We cannot process your claim until we have received it.

You can scan a copy of your letter and email it to:
redundancypaymentsonline@insolvency.gsi.gov.uk

Or you can post it to:

  • Redundancy Payments
  • PO BOX 16685
  • Birmingham
  • B2 2LX

Please include the following details on your award:

  • Your full name
  • Your day and month of birth
  • The last four characters of your national insurance number
