Verifying your identity

We need to verify your identity before we can make a payment.

You'll receive your payment faster if you verify your identity with GOV.UK Verify. With GOV.UK Verify you can also resume a claim you have already started.

To use GOV.UK Verify you'll need a UK passport, photocard driving license or bank statement.

Other ways to claim

You can use this service without using GOV.UK Verify.
